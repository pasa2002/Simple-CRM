Basketball CRM Overview

Dashboard

Player Experience Summary: Gain insights into the collective experience of your players, helping you assess team dynamics and performance.

Salary Overview: Keep track of player salaries to ensure financial transparency and effective budget management.

Investors Section: Manage your team's financial backers effortlessly. Add, edit, or delete investors to maintain accurate records of financial contributions.

Notes Summary: Access a consolidated view of all the notes associated with your players. This feature allows you to stay organized and informed about key player-related details.

Total Summary: A cumulative snapshot that provides a comprehensive view of the player experience, salary, investors, and notes, helping you make strategic decisions.

Investor Management

Add Investor: Easily add new investors to keep your financial records up-to-date.

Delete Investor: Remove outdated or irrelevant investor information as needed.

Edit Investor: Update investor details seamlessly, ensuring accurate and current information.

Notes

Write Notes: Jot down important information, observations, or reminders related to individual players.

Edit Notes: Update notes as situations evolve, ensuring that your information is always current.

Delete Notes: Remove outdated or irrelevant notes to maintain a streamlined and organized system.

Player Management

Add Player: Easily add new players to your roster, with information automatically sourced from the balldontlie API.

Edit Player: Keep player details accurate and up-to-date by making edits as needed.

Delete Player: Manage your roster effectively by removing players who are no longer part of the team.
